So what took so long?


One of the major reasons behind the large wait was due to inconsistant planning. ItsNerdy had made originally planned to release Universal Showdowns 10 days after the original trailer release date which was May 7th 2025. But due to some real-life issues with the team, we weren't able to keep up with the schedule. Nonetheless ItsNerdy promises it will not happen again by officially stating this:

"On behalf of Studio Nerds, I am immensely sorry for the delays, from now on we will have updates every week or so with big updates taking place every month."

Some other reasons for the long wait include the following: Not prepared enough, timezone difference for the team, computers breaking down, and ofcourse, reworks. Reworks took up a majority of the delays that happened, but these reworks were needed due to insufficient quality of some of the attacks. More reworks will be taking place through-out future updates. Artic Assasins ultimate will be slowly worked on in future updates aswell as Silent Wraith. As for the next character after all these tasks are completed has already been decided and will be announce in the near future.
